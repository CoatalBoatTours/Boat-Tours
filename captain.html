About Coastal Boat Tours

At Coastal Boat Tours, we offer unforgettable private charters through the stunning waters of Sarasota, Florida. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing sunset cruise, a full-day sandbar adventure, or a custom charter built just for your crew — we’ve got you covered.

Our boat is spacious, smooth, and ready to make memories. We specialize in good times, great views, and chill vibes. From dolphin watching and tiki bar hopping to romantic beach escapes, every trip is tailored to give you the perfect experience on the water.

Let’s make your Sarasota boat day legendary.
