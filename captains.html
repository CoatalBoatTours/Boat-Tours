Meet the Captains

Captain Cory Crouch

Meet Captain Cory Crouch – a true waterman at heart. With a deep love for fishing, nature, and all things outdoors, Cory brings passion and positive energy to every Coastal Boat Tour. He’s not just a skilled captain; he’s someone who genuinely loves showing people the magic of Sarasota’s waters.

When he’s not navigating the boat, you’ll find him soaking in sunsets, casting lines, or spending time with his loyal dog. Great with kids and known for his calm, friendly presence, Cory is the kind of captain you’ll want to tour with again and again.

Things Captain Cory loves:

  • Fisherman
  • Dog lover
  • Great with kids
  • Sunset chaser
  • Loves nature and animals
